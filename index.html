About Chatsubo MUD

Chatsubo MUD is a cyberpunk text-based multiplayer game set in the atmospheric world of William Gibson's Sprawl trilogy. Step into the gritty streets of Chiba City, where high tech meets low life, and the legendary Chatsubo bar serves as your gateway to adventure.

Immersive Cyberpunk World

Experience the dark future through rich storytelling and atmospheric descriptions inspired by classic cyberpunk literature.

Dynamic Character System

Choose from various roles including Console Cowboys, Street Samurai, Fixers, Techs, and Street Medics.

Syndicate Politics

Join player-created organizations, climb the ranks, and engage in the complex political landscape of the sprawl.

Continuous Development

Regular updates bring new features, improvements, and content based on community feedback.
